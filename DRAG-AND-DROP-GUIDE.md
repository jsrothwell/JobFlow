## ğŸ¯ How to Use Drag-and-Drop

### Quick Start

1. **Switch to Kanban View**
   - Click the "Kanban" tab in the sidebar

2. **Grab a Card**
   - Hover over any job card
   - Notice the hand icon appears (bottom right)
   - Click and hold on the card

3. **Drag the Card**
   - Move your mouse while holding
   - The card will become slightly smaller and transparent
   - Drop zones will highlight when you hover over them

4. **Drop the Card**
   - Move over any status column
   - The column border will light up
   - Release the mouse to drop
   - The job's status updates automatically!
